The Recur Platform

The Recur Ecosystem

Recur's ecosystem consists of three types of entities:

  • Enterprise Lenders: Storefront rental businesses with listings
  • Individual Lenders: Individual owners of equipment who post listings
  • Individual Renters: Individuals seeking to rent equipment

RentRecur brings these three parties together on one single platform to streamline the rentals process. What used to be a time-consuming and uncertain ordeal of searching, contacting, researching, and visiting rental offerers has been consolidated into a simple process of searchign for listings on the RentRecur website and reserving equipment on the spot.
